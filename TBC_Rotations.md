# TBC Class Rotations Reference

This document contains single-target and AoE rotation information for LibSpellDB tagging.
Sourced from Wowhead TBC Classic and Icy Veins TBC Classic guides (Feb 2026 audit).

## Classification System
- **ROTATIONAL**: Abilities used constantly in single-target rotation (Row 1)
- **DPS/HEAL/MAINTENANCE/AOE**: Throughput cooldowns, buffs to maintain, AoE abilities (Row 2)
- **SITUATIONAL**: Big cooldowns, utility, conditional abilities (Row 2/3)

## Warrior

### Fury ST Rotation
1. Bloodthirst (on CD)
2. Whirlwind (on CD)
3. Rampage (maintain buff — key to sustain)
4. Heroic Strike (rage dump when >50 rage, queued on next swing)
5. Execute (<20%)

### Fury Situational
- Death Wish (big CD)
- Recklessness (big CD)
- Sweeping Strikes (AoE)
- Cleave (AoE rage dump)
- Bloodrage (resource generation)

### Arms ST Rotation
1. Mortal Strike (on CD)
2. Overpower (when proc — highest priority when available)
3. Whirlwind (on CD)
4. Rend (maintain on bleed-vulnerable targets)
5. Slam (filler between swings)
6. Heroic Strike (rage dump)
7. Execute (<20%)

### Arms Situational
- Death Wish (big CD)
- Recklessness (big CD)
- Sweeping Strikes (AoE)
- Bloodrage (resource generation)

### Protection ST Rotation
1. Shield Block (maintain for crush immunity)
2. Revenge (on proc — highest TPS ability)
3. Shield Slam (on CD)
4. Devastate (filler / sunder stacks)
5. Thunder Clap (maintain debuff)
6. Heroic Strike (rage dump)

### Protection Situational
- Shield Wall (major defensive CD)
- Last Stand (emergency HP)
- Bloodrage (resource generation)
- Spell Reflection (caster fights)
- Commanding Shout / Battle Shout (maintain)

## Rogue

### Combat ST Rotation
1. Slice and Dice (maintain — #1 priority, even at 1 CP)
2. Sinister Strike (builder)
3. Rupture (maintain at 5 CP if target lives long enough)
4. Eviscerate (CP dump when SnD and Rupture are up)

### Assassination ST Rotation
1. Slice and Dice (maintain)
2. Mutilate (builder)
3. Rupture (maintain at 4-5 CP)
4. Envenom (finisher, replaces Eviscerate)

### Subtlety ST Rotation
1. Slice and Dice (maintain)
2. Hemorrhage (builder)
3. Rupture (maintain)
4. Eviscerate (finisher)

### Rogue Situational
- Blade Flurry (big CD / cleave — also used on CD in Combat)
- Adrenaline Rush (big CD)
- Cold Blood (big CD)
- Expose Armor (if no warrior for Sunder)
- Shiv (poison application — Assassination)

## Mage

### Arcane ST Rotation
1. Arcane Blast (stack debuff to 3)
2. Arcane Missiles (to dump/reset AB stacks, or filler)
3. Arcane Power (burn phase — use with Presence of Mind)
4. Presence of Mind (instant-cast AB or AM)
5. Evocation (mana recovery after burn phase)

**Burn/Conserve cycle**: Burn with AB spam during Arcane Power + trinkets, then conserve mana with lower AB stacks + AM procs until cooldowns return. Mana Gems used during burn phases.

### Fire ST Rotation
1. Fireball (main nuke — spam)
2. Scorch (maintain Improved Scorch debuff if assigned)
3. Combustion (big CD — guaranteed crits)

Note: Pyroblast is NOT used in rotation (6s cast, no instant proc in TBC).

### Frost ST Rotation
1. Frostbolt (main nuke — spam)
2. Summon Water Elemental (on CD)
3. Ice Lance (only after Frost Nova or on frozen targets — shatter combo)
4. Icy Veins (big CD — haste)
5. Cold Snap (reset Icy Veins + Water Elemental)

Note: Fingers of Frost does NOT exist in TBC. Ice Lance is only used in shatter combos.

### Mage Situational
- Arcane Power (burn phase CD)
- Icy Veins (haste CD)
- Combustion (crit CD)
- Evocation (mana recovery — Arcane)
- Blizzard (AoE)
- Flamestrike (AoE)
- Arcane Explosion (AoE)
- Cone of Cold (AoE / shatter combo)
- Mana Gem (mana recovery — Arcane)

## Warlock

### Affliction ST Rotation

**Unstable Affliction build (with pet):**
1. Curse of Doom (if boss lives 60s+) or Curse of Agony (short fights)
2. Unstable Affliction (maintain — has cast time)
3. Corruption (maintain — let expire before refreshing)
4. Siphon Life (maintain)
5. Immolate (maintain if time permits)
6. Shadow Bolt (filler)
7. Life Tap (mana recovery, during movement)
8. Drain Soul (<25% for Soul Shards)

**Demonic Sacrifice build (sacrifice Succubus for +15% Shadow):**
1. Assigned Curse (Doom or Agony)
2. Corruption (maintain)
3. Siphon Life (maintain)
4. Shadow Bolt (filler)
5. Life Tap (mana recovery)

### Demonology ST Rotation
1. Corruption (maintain)
2. Immolate (maintain)
3. Assigned Curse (Doom, Elements, or Recklessness)
4. Shadow Bolt (filler — maintain Improved Shadow Bolt debuff)
5. Life Tap (mana recovery)

Key: Keep Felguard alive at all costs (significant DPS portion via Demonic Knowledge).

### Destruction ST Rotation

**Shadow Destruction (sacrifice Succubus for +15% Shadow):**
1. Assigned Curse (Doom or Elements)
2. Immolate (maintain)
3. Shadow Bolt (filler)
4. Life Tap (mana recovery)

**Fire Destruction (sacrifice Imp for +15% Fire):**
1. Assigned Curse
2. Immolate (maintain)
3. Incinerate (filler — replaces Shadow Bolt)
4. Life Tap (mana recovery)

Note: Conflagrate is NOT used in PvE rotation (consumes Immolate, DPS loss). Shadowburn for movement/burst only.

### Warlock Situational
- Seed of Corruption (AoE — primary)
- Rain of Fire (AoE)
- Hellfire (AoE — damages self)
- Shadowfury (AoE stun — Destruction)
- Shadowburn (instant burst, costs Soul Shard)
- Amplify Curse (Affliction — boost CoA/CoD)
- Life Tap (mana management — all specs)

## Hunter

All hunter specs share the core mechanic of **shot weaving**: alternating Steady Shot between Auto Shots without clipping autos. Use a swing timer addon.

### Beast Mastery ST Rotation
1. Auto Shot (never clip)
2. Steady Shot (weave between autos — 1:1 ratio)
3. Kill Command (on CD — does NOT trigger GCD)
4. Multi-Shot (on CD — higher damage than Steady Shot even single-target)
5. Bestial Wrath (on CD or save for burn phase)
6. Serpent Sting (maintain on long fights)

### Marksmanship ST Rotation
1. Auto Shot (never clip)
2. Steady Shot (weave between autos)
3. Aimed Shot (opener only — too slow for regular rotation)
4. Multi-Shot (on CD)
5. Kill Command (on CD — no GCD)
6. Arcane Shot (weave in when mana permits)
7. Serpent Sting (maintain)

### Survival ST Rotation
1. Auto Shot (never clip)
2. Steady Shot (weave between autos — 1:1.5 rotation most common)
3. Multi-Shot (on CD)
4. Kill Command (on CD — no GCD)
5. Raptor Strike (melee weaving — advanced, small DPS gain)

Key: Survival is brought primarily for Expose Weakness (passive talent — AP debuff on target from crits, boosting all physical DPS).

### Hunter Situational
- Bestial Wrath (big CD — BM)
- Rapid Fire (haste CD)
- Readiness (reset all CDs — use after Rapid Fire + Bestial Wrath)
- Misdirection (threat redirect on pull)
- Volley (AoE)
- Multi-Shot (AoE / cleave)
- Serpent Sting (DoT maintenance)
- Explosive Trap (AoE — only worth on 7+ targets)

## Priest

### Shadow ST Rotation
1. Vampiric Touch (maintain — mana battery for party)
2. Shadow Word: Pain (maintain — high damage per cast time)
3. Vampiric Embrace (maintain on target — party healing)
4. Mind Blast (on CD)
5. Shadow Word: Death (instant nuke — careful of self-damage backlash)
6. Mind Flay (filler — clip after 2 ticks when higher priority spells available)

### Discipline (Healing)
1. Prayer of Mending (on CD — on target expected to take damage)
2. Power Word: Shield (emergency — prevent death before heal lands)
3. Greater Heal (main heal — downrank for efficiency)
4. Flash Heal (urgent single-target)
5. Prayer of Healing (3+ party members damaged)
6. Binding Heal (you + ally both need healing)
7. Renew (sustained damage on tanks)

### Holy (Healing)
1. Circle of Healing (instant AoE heal — defining Holy ability)
2. Prayer of Mending (on CD)
3. Prayer of Healing (3+ party members damaged)
4. Flash Heal (urgent single-target)
5. Greater Heal (big single-target — downrank for efficiency)
6. Renew (sustained damage)
7. Binding Heal (you + ally)

### Priest Situational
- Shadowfiend (mana regen — 5 min CD, all specs)
- Inner Focus (free spell + crit — pair with expensive heal or SWP)
- Pain Suppression (Disc — external damage reduction CD)
- Power Infusion (Disc — haste buff, use on caster DPS or self)
- Fear Ward (pre-buff on assigned target)
- Lightwell (Holy — powerful if people click it)

## Paladin

### Retribution ST Rotation
1. Seal of Blood (Horde) / Seal of the Martyr (Alliance) — maintain
2. Crusader Strike (on CD)
3. Judgement (on CD)
4. **Seal Twisting** (advanced): Cast Seal of Command, swap to Seal of Blood in final 0.4s before swing to proc both (~13% DPS increase)
5. Consecration (if mana permits — expensive filler)
6. Exorcism (vs Undead/Demons)
7. Hammer of Wrath (<20%)

### Protection ST Rotation
1. Holy Shield (maintain — crush immunity on crush-capable bosses)
2. Consecration (on CD — main threat generator)
3. Seal of Righteousness (maintain)
4. Judgement (on CD)
5. Avenger's Shield (pull / on CD)
6. Exorcism (vs Undead/Demons)

Note: Mana management is the key limiting factor. Use Seal of Wisdom for sustain on long fights.

### Holy (Healing)
1. Holy Light (primary heal — downrank for efficiency)
2. Flash of Light (efficient heal for lighter damage)
3. Holy Shock (instant — emergency / movement)
4. Divine Favor + max-rank Holy Light (guaranteed crit combo)
5. Divine Illumination (50% mana cost reduction — use on CD or pre-heavy damage)
6. Judgement of Light / Wisdom on boss (passive raid healing/mana)

Note: Holy Paladin is a single-target healer in TBC. No AoE healing tools.

### Paladin Situational
- Avenging Wrath (30% damage increase — 2 min CD)
- Divine Favor (guaranteed crit heal — 2 min CD)
- Divine Illumination (50% mana reduction — 3 min CD)
- Hammer of Wrath (execute <20%)
- Consecration (AoE threat/damage)
- Lay on Hands (emergency full heal)
- Divine Shield (immunity)

## Druid

### Balance ST Rotation
1. Faerie Fire (maintain — 3% hit for physical DPS via Improved FF)
2. Moonfire (maintain DoT)
3. Force of Nature (on CD — if talented)
4. Starfire (main nuke — spam)
5. Insect Swarm (movement-only — instant cast while moving)
6. Wrath (shorter cast — movement/filler)

Note: Balance is valued primarily as a support spec (Faerie Fire, Innervate, combat res). On mana-constrained fights, drop DoTs and just spam Starfire.

### Feral Cat ST Rotation
1. Mangle (Cat) (maintain debuff — 30% bleed bonus)
2. Shred (primary builder — requires behind target)
3. Rake (maintain DoT)
4. Rip (maintain at 4-5 CP — if target lives through full duration)
5. Ferocious Bite (dump CP if target will die before Rip duration)
6. Faerie Fire (Feral) (if no Balance Druid providing debuff)

**Powershifting**: Shift out of Cat Form and back to regain energy via Furor talent. Core to the rotation for energy management. Requires mana sustain (Innervate, Dark Runes, potions).

Note: Tiger's Fury is NOT rotational — only use at 100 energy during pull or forced downtime.

### Feral Bear ST Rotation
1. Mangle (Bear) (on CD)
2. Lacerate (build to 5 stacks, then refresh)
3. Swipe (filler — surpasses Lacerate at ~2700+ raid-buffed AP)
4. Maul (rage dump — queued on next swing)

### Feral Bear AoE
1. Swipe (primary AoE threat)
2. Maul (rage dump)
3. Tab-target Mangle (spread threat)

### Restoration (Healing)
1. Lifebloom (maintain 3 stacks on tank — refresh at ~6s)
2. Rejuvenation (maintain on tank + raid members taking damage)
3. Regrowth (direct heal + HoT when needed)
4. Swiftmend (emergency burst — consumes Rejuvenation or Regrowth, NOT Lifebloom)
5. Nature's Swiftness + max-rank Healing Touch (critical emergency)

Note: Tree of Life form restricts spell usage — must drop form for Healing Touch/Tranquility.

### Druid Situational
- Force of Nature (treants — Balance)
- Tiger's Fury (energy CD — NOT rotational, pull/downtime only)
- Innervate (mana regen — self or ally)
- Barkskin (damage reduction, anti-pushback for Hurricane)
- Hurricane (AoE)
- Swipe (AoE — Bear only)
- Tranquility (emergency party heal — Resto)
- Rebirth (combat res)

## Shaman

### Elemental ST Rotation
1. Lightning Bolt x3 (main nuke)
2. Chain Lightning (on CD — fits between 3 LB casts)
3. Repeat: 3x Lightning Bolt → 1x Chain Lightning cycle
4. Flame Shock (movement-only — instant cast while moving)
5. Earth Shock (only on targets dying before a cast can complete)

**With haste/Bloodlust**: Fit 4x Lightning Bolt between Chain Lightnings.

**Pre-pull**: Activate Elemental Mastery for guaranteed crit first cast.

### Elemental AoE
1. Fire Nova Totem (on CD)
2. Chain Lightning (on CD)
3. Magma Totem (sustained AoE between Fire Nova CDs)
4. Lightning Bolt (filler)

### Enhancement ST Rotation
1. Stormstrike (on CD)
2. Flame Shock (maintain DoT)
3. Earth Shock (when Flame Shock has 6+ seconds remaining)
4. Searing Totem (maintain for single target)
5. Auto-attack with Windfury Weapon (main hand — 3s internal CD on procs)

**Totem Twisting** (advanced): Drop Windfury Totem, then swap to Grace of Air Totem during the 10s window that Windfury's effect remains active, providing both buffs simultaneously to the melee group.

### Restoration (Healing)
1. Earth Shield (maintain on tank — heals on hit, pushback reduction)
2. Water Shield (maintain on self — MP5 + mana on hit)
3. Chain Heal (primary raid heal — downrank for efficiency: Rank 1-3 light damage, Rank 5 heavy)
4. Healing Wave (single-target — build Healing Way stacks with Rank 1, then use higher ranks)
5. Lesser Healing Wave (emergency only — fast but mana-inefficient)
6. Nature's Swiftness + max-rank Healing Wave (instant emergency heal)

### Shaman Situational
- Bloodlust / Heroism (raid-wide haste — coordinate with raid)
- Elemental Mastery (guaranteed crit — Elemental)
- Shamanistic Rage (mana regen + damage reduction — Enhancement)
- Mana Tide Totem (party mana recovery — Restoration)
- Nature's Swiftness (instant-cast heal — Restoration)
- Fire Nova Totem (AoE)
- Chain Lightning (AoE / cleave)
- Totem of Wrath (spell hit/crit — Elemental)
